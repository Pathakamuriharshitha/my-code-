India's Historical Marvels

Experience the artistry and stories etched in stone

Ajanta Caves, Maharashtra

Ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves dating from the 2nd century BCE, famed for murals and serenity.

Konark Sun Temple, Odisha

13th-century chariot-shaped temple dedicated to the Sun God, an engineering and artistic marvel.

Jaisalmer Fort, Rajasthan

Also known as Sonar Quila, this golden sandstone fort is one of the last inhabited forts in the world.
